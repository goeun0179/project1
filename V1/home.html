NEW SIGNAGE

Designing a wayfinding system for the RISD Graduate building.
This was inspired by the traces of spray paint in the building’s stairwell left behind by students. I found that the spray paintings were expression of creativeness and freedom which represents the spirit of RISD community. Therefore, I used the spray-painted aesthetic as a main element of the new signage. The project intends to bring a dynamism to the building and to change the perspective that the atmosphere is bland.
Goeun Park, 2016
